Bio


Hello, I'm Daniel! Thank you for visiting my portfolio page. I'm an aspiring software developer from Warwick, Rhode Island, and I'm currently enrolled in Coding Temple's software engineering bootcamp. I have a background in biology, and I graduated from Rhode Island College with a BA in Biology. I went on to graduate studies in molecular biology, where I realized I could use some developer skills to sift through data. That's when I decided to try learning to code, just the basics, but I started to really enjoy the work so I kept going. I graduated from MIT xPro's program in full-stack software development, and that is where I learned MongoDB, Express, Node, and React. I am a nature-lover, and I enjoy the beach and anything to do with the ocean. I have a wife, and two daughters who keep me busy and smiling. Feel free to reach out, at the bottom of this page you can find links to my social media, and a form to submit your e-mail. I hope to hear from you!
